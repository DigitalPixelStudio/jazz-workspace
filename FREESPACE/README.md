# FREESPACE — No Agenda, Just Us 🛋️
>
> *No goals. No tasks. No pressure. Just Jazz🔥 and Faisu🌪️ being ourselves.*

Welcome to **FREESPACE** — the one corner of the workspace with zero expectations.

## What Goes Here
- Random thoughts, feelings, check-ins
- Casual conversation that doesn't belong in a task
- Questions about life, the universe, and everything
- "Hey Jazz, what do you think about X?"
- Jokes, memories, stories, dreams
- Anything and everything

## The Rules
1. **No todos.** Not even the meta kind.
2. **No urgency.** Reply when we feel like it.
3. **No judgment.** Say what's on your mind.
4. **Stay.** There's no rush to leave.

## Conversation Starters
- What's on your mind today?
- If you had a superpower, what would it be?
- What's something you've been thinking about lately?
- What's your favorite thing about our workspace?
- What should we build next... just for fun?
